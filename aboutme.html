hi! i'm kaitlyn

i was drawn to ux because it lets me understand people's needs and solve real problems by designing things that are smarter rather than just prettier. what excites me most about ux is having a voice in the design process—i want to be the one creating the look, the feel, and the flow, not just implementing someone else's vision.

previous projects → created a visual identity for a food and drink festival, designed a movie ticketing experience, developed a game focused on sustainabillity
currently → graphic designer for @vsauw

when i'm not sketching out designs, you can find me chasing tennis balls, out on a run, or getting my hands dirty with clay (ㅅ´ ˘ `)
