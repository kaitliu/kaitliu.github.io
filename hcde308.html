Overview

The design challenge was to design a visual identity for a festival on the University of Washington campus. We were given 10 weeks to create a wordmark, poster, app, etc. each separated into weekly briefs or exercises. I chose to focus on making a food and drink festival that help food lovers discover hidden gems around Seattle. My core value proposition is to help foodies rise above the usual food scene and be more exploratory. The unique selling point of this festival is that unlike typical large scale food events, this festival provides an area where smaller and opening businesses can gain more exposure and food food enthusiasts to find a niche set of vendors and smaller businesses waiting to be discovered.
