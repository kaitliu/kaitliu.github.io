Overview

As part of a Directed Research Group (DRG) at the University of Washington, I designed and developed a 2D narrative simulation game in GameMaker focused on shoreline sustainability and marine life restoration. Players restore a forgotten coast through tide-based gameplay cycles that shift between exploration, cleanup, and environmental care. The game combines environmental storytelling with soft pixel art to promote awareness of ocean pollution and our connection to coastal ecosystems.
